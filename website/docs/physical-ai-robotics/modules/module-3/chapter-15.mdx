## Lesson: Sensing and Perception Hardware for Physical AI

### Introduction to Sensing in Physical AI

Sensing and perception hardware form the foundation of a humanoid robot's ability to understand and interact with its environment. Unlike traditional AI systems that process digital information, Physical AI must extract meaningful information from real-world sensory data, which is often noisy, incomplete, and ambiguous. The sensing system enables the robot to perceive humans, objects, surfaces, sounds, and environmental conditions, making intelligent behavior possible.

The key requirements for sensing hardware in Physical AI include:
- **Real-time Processing**: Fast response for interactive applications
- **Accuracy and Precision**: Reliable information extraction
- **Robustness**: Operation in varying conditions
- **Integration**: Coordination of multiple sensing modalities
- **Safety**: Safe operation with humans and objects
- **Efficiency**: Low power consumption for extended operation
