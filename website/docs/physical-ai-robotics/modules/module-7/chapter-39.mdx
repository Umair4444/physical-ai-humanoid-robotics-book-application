## Lesson: Tactile and Haptic Sensing Technologies

### Introduction to Tactile and Haptic Sensing

Tactile and haptic sensing technologies form a critical component of Physical AI, enabling humanoid robots to perceive and interact with their environment through touch. Unlike visual or auditory sensing, tactile sensing provides intimate physical interaction information, including force, pressure, texture, temperature, and slip detection. Haptic technologies extend this to include active haptic feedback, allowing robots to both sense touch and provide tactile feedback to humans.

The essential roles of tactile and haptic sensing in humanoid robots include:
- **Manipulation**: Providing feedback for dexterous object handling
- **Safety**: Ensuring safe interaction with humans and objects
- **Material Recognition**: Identifying object properties through touch
- **Compliance**: Enabling flexible and adaptive interaction
- **Communication**: Providing tactile feedback for human-robot interaction
- **Quality Control**: Monitoring grasp stability and manipulation success
