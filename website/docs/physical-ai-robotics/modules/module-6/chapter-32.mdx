## Lesson: Dynamic Balance and Posture Control in Physical AI

### Introduction to Dynamic Balance Control

Dynamic balance and posture control in Physical AI represent fundamental capabilities that enable humanoid robots to maintain stability while performing complex tasks and navigating challenging environments. Unlike static balance which maintains equilibrium in stationary positions, dynamic balance involves continuous adjustment during movement and in response to external disturbances.

The core challenges in dynamic balance control include:
- **Multi-Domain Coordination**: Integrating sensory, motor, and cognitive processes
- **Real-Time Response**: Reacting to disturbances within biological time constants
- **Uncertainty Management**: Handling sensor noise, model inaccuracies, and environmental changes
- **Energy Efficiency**: Maintaining balance with minimal energy expenditure
- **Safety Assurance**: Ensuring stable recovery from disturbances
- **Human-Like Behavior**: Achieving natural, human-like balance responses
