## Lesson: Cognitive Architectures and Planning Systems in Physical AI

### Introduction to Cognitive Architectures in Physical AI

Cognitive architectures in Physical AI provide comprehensive frameworks that organize the various components needed for intelligent behavior in embodied systems. These architectures integrate perception, reasoning, learning, memory, and action selection into unified systems that can exhibit flexible, adaptive, and purposeful behavior in complex real-world environments.

The key challenges for cognitive architectures in Physical AI include:
- **Embodied Cognition**: Understanding how intelligence emerges from the interaction between an agent and its physical environment
- **Real-Time Processing**: Managing complex cognitive processes within strict timing constraints
- **Sensorimotor Integration**: Coordinating perception and action in real-time
- **Memory Management**: Handling different types of memory (sensory, working, long-term) efficiently
- **Planning and Execution**: Coordinating high-level goals with low-level actions
- **Adaptation**: Adjusting behavior based on experience and environmental changes
