## Lesson: Human-Robot Interaction Design and Principles

### Introduction to Human-Robot Interaction

Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) is a multidisciplinary field that focuses on the design, development, and evaluation of robots intended to interact with humans. In the context of humanoid robotics, HRI principles are particularly important as these robots are specifically designed to operate in human environments and interact with human users in natural, intuitive ways.

The fundamental aspects of HRI include:
- **Communication**: How robots and humans exchange information
- **Collaboration**: How robots and humans work together
- **Trust**: How humans perceive and rely on robotic systems
- **Safety**: How robots ensure safe interaction with humans
- **Acceptance**: How humans adopt and integrate robots into their lives
- **Social Norms**: How robots conform to human social expectations
