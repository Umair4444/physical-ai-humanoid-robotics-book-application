## Lesson: Advanced Motion Planning for Humanoid Robots

### Introduction to Advanced Motion Planning

Advanced motion planning for humanoid robots involves sophisticated algorithms and methodologies to generate feasible, safe, and efficient trajectories that account for the robot's complex kinematics, dynamics, balance constraints, and interaction with the environment. Unlike simple path planning, humanoid motion planning must consider high-dimensional configuration spaces, dynamic balance requirements, and multi-objective optimization while operating in real-time with environmental uncertainties.

The core challenges in advanced humanoid motion planning include:
- **High-Dimensional Motion Planning**: Managing 30+ degrees of freedom efficiently
- **Dynamic Balance Constraints**: Ensuring stable locomotion throughout planned motions
- **Real-Time Performance**: Computing plans within strict timing constraints
- **Multi-Objective Optimization**: Balancing competing requirements (stability, efficiency, safety)
- **Environmental Adaptation**: Handling dynamic and uncertain environments
- **Human Safety**: Ensuring collision-free and safe interaction with humans
